Reducing Plastic Pollution

Our goal is to reduce the environmental impact of cities by tackling plastic pollution in the ocean.

Marine wildlife faces harm from plastic in the ocean as they can mistake it for food or become entangled.

How does plastic get in the ocean? Wind and rain wash plastic litter into rivers and the ocean.
