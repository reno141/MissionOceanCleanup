The Challenge

Did you know that 80% of ocean plastic comes from land, and cities play a huge role?

Pollution is when something makes the environment unhealthy. The environment is everything around us, like the air we breathe, the water we drink and the land we live on.

Pollution from cities can harm the ocean and the creatures living in it. Plastic bags, bottles and other rubbish can end up in rivers and eventually in the ocean.

By reducing waste, recycling right, and choosing eco-friendly options, we can rewrite the story.
